Introduction - What is Paw Park?

In the last 5 years, 15% of pet guardians reported a lost dog or cat. Every pet lost not only causes harm for the pet but creates a void in the heart of the owner. Width Paw Park, we can help you locate missing dogs! With our two main features, Adopt a Paw and Lost and Found, you can see if anyone has located your precious pet or you can have the option to browse through our database and see if you'd like to give a home to one of the pets there! We are committed to our mission of making sure every dog has a home to come to and hope you can help fulfill it.
